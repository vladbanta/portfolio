Fan Theories

Explore some of the most interesting and mind-blowing fan theories about your favorite movies!

The Dark Knight - Joker's True Identity

Theory: The Joker was a former soldier suffering from PTSD, which explains his combat skills and strategic thinking.

Why It Might Be True: His knowledge of weapons, explosives, and psychological warfare suggests military experience.

Inception - Cobb Never Woke Up

Theory: Cobb was trapped in a dream the entire time, and the ending scene was just another level of his subconscious.

Why It Might Be True: The spinning top wobbles, but never fully stops, leaving the reality ambiguous.

Interstellar - Cooper Died in the Black Hole

Theory: Cooper never survived Gargantua and everything after was his mind creating closure before death.

Why It Might Be True: The laws of physics break down inside a black hole, so his survival seems impossible.

Fight Club - The Narrator is Already Dead

Theory: The Narrator was actually dead the whole time, and everything was his dying hallucination.

Why It Might Be True: Many moments in the movie suggest an unreliable narrator and distorted reality.
